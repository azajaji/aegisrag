Production databases are backed up incrementally every hour and fully every night. The retention window is 30 days for hourly backups, 90 days for daily backups, and 1 year for monthly backups.

Restores from backup must be requested via a 'Restore Request' ticket and approved by a service owner. Restore time objective is four hours for critical systems and 24 hours for standard systems.

Personal device backups are not provided by IT. Users are encouraged to use the university OneDrive sync client for documents, which provides 1 TB of synchronized storage per user.

Test restores are performed quarterly for at least one critical system to validate backup integrity. The test results are documented in the backup operations log.
