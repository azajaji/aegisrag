Passwords must be at least 12 characters long and contain a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, digits, and symbols.

Passwords expire every 180 days. Users receive reminder emails 14, 7, and 1 day before expiration.

The system prevents reuse of any of the past 12 passwords. Passwords that have appeared in known breach corpora are also rejected.

Account lockout occurs after five consecutive failed authentication attempts within 15 minutes. Lockout is automatically lifted after 30 minutes or can be cleared by IT support after identity verification.

Self-service password reset is available through the identity portal using either an enrolled phone number or a backup email. Both methods require a one-time code valid for 10 minutes.
