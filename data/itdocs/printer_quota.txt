Each undergraduate student receives a printing quota of 500 pages per semester. Graduate students receive 1,000 pages per semester. Quotas reset on the first day of each semester.

Color printing counts as three pages against the quota for each color page printed. Duplex printing counts as one page per side.

Additional pages may be purchased through the cashier office in 100-page increments. The price is 5 SAR per 100 pages for monochrome printing.

Print jobs are held in the print queue for two hours before being deleted unreleased. Releasing a job requires tapping the campus ID card on the printer reader.
