The 'UnivWiFi-Secure' network uses WPA2-Enterprise with PEAP authentication. Select this network for the best performance and security on personal devices.

The 'UnivWiFi-Guest' network is open but rate-limited to 5 Mbps per device and requires daily reauthentication through the captive portal.

Eduroam is supported across all campus locations. Connect using your full university email address and your standard university password.

Wi-Fi coverage map and known dead zones are listed in the IT knowledge base article 'WIFI-COV-23' which is updated quarterly.

Reporting persistent connection issues requires the MAC address of the affected device. This can be found in the network settings of the device under 'Wi-Fi Address' or 'Physical Address'.
