Remote Desktop access to university workstations is provided through the Remote Workstation Gateway, accessible only from on the VPN.

Sessions are limited to four concurrent users per workstation and are terminated after one hour of idle time without keyboard or mouse activity.

File transfer between the remote workstation and the local device is blocked by default; exceptions can be requested for specific projects via the IT portal.

Audio is disabled on remote workstations by default. Re-enabling audio requires user-level configuration but is not supported by the IT helpdesk.

Recording of remote sessions is performed for compliance audits in research-data-classified environments; users are notified at the time of login.
