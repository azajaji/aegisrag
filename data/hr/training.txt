All new hires complete a mandatory orientation program within their first four weeks of employment. The orientation covers HR policies, IT security, and compliance training.

Each employee is entitled to up to forty hours of professional development per fiscal year. Approval is at the supervisor's discretion based on relevance to the role.

Tuition reimbursement is available for degree programs aligned with the employee's role, up to 30,000 SAR per fiscal year. A minimum grade of B is required for reimbursement.

Mandatory annual refresher courses include data protection, anti-harassment, and information security. Completion is tracked through the learning management system.

Failure to complete mandatory training by the deadline results in a reminder to the employee and supervisor. Continued non-completion after thirty days is reflected in the next performance review.
