Resignations require a written notice period of 30 days for staff and one full semester for faculty. Shorter notice may be accepted with the supervisor's written consent.

End-of-service indemnity is calculated at one-half month's salary per year of service for the first five years and one month per year of service thereafter.

Exit interviews are conducted by HR within the final week of employment. Participation is voluntary but encouraged.

Final paycheck, including accrued leave and end-of-service indemnity, is issued no later than 30 calendar days after the last day of employment.

Termination for cause does not entitle the employee to end-of-service indemnity unless overturned by the grievance committee.
