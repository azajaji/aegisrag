Eligible staff may request a remote work arrangement of up to two days per week subject to operational needs and supervisor approval.

Remote work agreements are renewed every six months and may be revoked with two weeks' notice if performance or operational needs change.

Equipment provided for remote work is limited to a university-managed laptop and a one-time 1,500 SAR home-office allowance.

Time tracking for non-exempt remote employees is required through the official timekeeping system. Overtime rules apply identically to remote and on-site work.

Remote work outside the kingdom requires written approval and may have tax or compliance implications that the employee is responsible for understanding.
