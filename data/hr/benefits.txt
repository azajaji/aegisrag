Full-time staff are enrolled in the university medical plan from their first day of employment. The plan covers the employee, spouse, and dependent children up to age 26.

The retirement contribution is 9 percent of base salary paid by the employer plus a matching 5 percent contribution by the employee.

Employees with five or more years of continuous service receive 50 percent tuition remission for their dependents enrolling in undergraduate programs.

Life insurance equal to two times annual base salary is provided automatically. Additional coverage may be purchased at group rates.

Hajj leave of up to fifteen days is granted once during an employee's tenure, contingent on six months of prior service.
