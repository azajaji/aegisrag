Full-time staff are entitled to 30 working days of annual leave per fiscal year. Faculty on twelve-month contracts receive 25 working days; faculty on academic-year contracts do not accrue annual leave.

Annual leave is accrued at a rate of 2.5 days per month of completed service for staff. New hires may use accrued leave only after completing three months of continuous employment.

Annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least ten business days in advance and require approval from the direct supervisor. Emergency leave with less notice requires written justification.

Up to ten unused leave days may be carried over into the following fiscal year. Excess unused leave is forfeited and is not paid out, except upon termination of employment.

On termination of employment, accrued but unused leave is paid out at the employee's base salary rate, up to a maximum of forty days.
