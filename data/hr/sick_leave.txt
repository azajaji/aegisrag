Full-time employees are entitled to 30 days of paid sick leave per calendar year. Sick leave does not roll over between years.

Sick leave of more than three consecutive days requires a medical certificate from an accredited physician. The certificate must be uploaded to the HR portal within five business days of the return date.

An additional 60 days of half-pay sick leave may be granted for serious or chronic conditions, subject to review by the Occupational Health office.

Employees who require more than 90 days of consecutive sick leave may apply for an extended medical leave of absence of up to one year. Position is held only for the first six months.

Sick leave abuse, including patterns of single-day absences adjacent to weekends, may trigger a fitness-for-duty review and potential disciplinary action.
