Base salaries are paid monthly on the 27th of each month. When the 27th falls on a weekend, payment is made on the preceding business day.

Overtime is paid at 1.5 times the hourly rate for hours worked beyond 48 in a week. Overtime requires advance approval from the direct supervisor.

Annual bonuses, where awarded, are typically paid in December and are calibrated based on individual performance ratings and overall university financial performance.

Cost-of-living adjustments are reviewed every January by the Board of Trustees. There is no automatic cost-of-living escalator.

Salary advances of up to two months of base pay may be requested in writing for documented hardship. Advances are repaid through equal deductions across the following six months.
