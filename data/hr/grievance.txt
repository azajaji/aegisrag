Employees may file a grievance with HR for matters including disciplinary action, denial of promotion, or violations of policy. Grievances must be filed within thirty days of the triggering event.

Grievance review proceeds through three stages: HR triage, departmental review by the head of the relevant unit, and final review by the grievance committee.

The grievance committee is composed of five members: two HR representatives, two peer employees, and one external arbitrator.

Decisions of the grievance committee are binding on the university and may not be further appealed except in matters involving alleged criminal conduct.

The university does not retaliate against employees who file grievances in good faith, regardless of the outcome.
