All open positions are posted on the university careers portal for at least fifteen business days before applications close.

Internal candidates have priority consideration when their qualifications are at least equivalent to the strongest external candidate. Internal applications must be received within the first five days of the posting.

Background checks are performed on all final candidates for academic and administrative positions. Faculty positions additionally require verification of educational credentials by an external agency.

Reference checks must include at least two professional references from the candidate's most recent five years of employment. References are conducted by phone and documented in writing.

Offer letters are valid for ten business days from the date of signing by the Provost or Chief Operating Officer. Acceptance after this period requires reissuance.
