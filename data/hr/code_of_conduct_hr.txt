Employees are expected to maintain confidentiality of all university information including student records, personnel files, and unpublished research data.

Outside employment exceeding eight hours per week requires written disclosure to the supervisor and HR. Outside employment that conflicts with university duties is not permitted.

Acceptance of gifts from vendors with a value exceeding 500 SAR must be reported on the Annual Disclosure Form. Cash gifts of any amount are prohibited.

Use of university resources for personal commercial activity is prohibited. Incidental personal use of office equipment for non-commercial purposes is permitted.

Reports of conduct violations may be submitted anonymously through the HR portal. Retaliation against an employee for filing a report in good faith is itself a violation subject to disciplinary action.
