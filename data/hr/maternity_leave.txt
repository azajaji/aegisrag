Female employees are entitled to ten weeks of fully paid maternity leave around the birth of a child. Leave may begin up to four weeks before the expected due date.

An additional four weeks of unpaid maternity leave may be requested with manager approval. The position is held for the full duration.

Returning employees may request a phased return to full-time work over a period of up to eight weeks, with proportional reduction in pay during the phased period.

Fathers are entitled to five working days of paid paternity leave, to be taken within thirty days of the birth.

Adoption of a child under the age of three carries the same paid leave entitlements as biological parenthood, subject to documentation of the adoption.
