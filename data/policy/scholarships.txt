The Merit Excellence Scholarship covers 100 percent of tuition for undergraduate students with a cumulative GPA of 3.75 or higher and is renewable annually.

The Need-Based Aid Program provides up to 25,000 SAR per semester to students who demonstrate financial need through the standardized application. The application must be submitted by April 15 for the following academic year.

Graduate Research Assistantships provide a monthly stipend of 4,500 SAR plus tuition waiver in exchange for twenty hours of research work per week. Assistantships are renewed each semester subject to satisfactory performance.

The Faculty Sponsored Travel Award covers conference travel up to 8,000 SAR per student per academic year. Applications must be submitted at least sixty days before the conference.

International Student Scholarships are limited to twenty awards per year, prioritize STEM disciplines, and require maintenance of a 3.5 GPA for renewal.
