Students in good academic standing may request a leave of absence of up to two consecutive semesters without losing enrolled status.

Leave-of-absence requests are submitted through the dean of students and require a documented reason and an anticipated return date.

Time on leave does not count toward the maximum time allowed to complete a degree program.

Students returning from leave must contact the registrar at least 60 days before the start of the semester to confirm their return and reactivate their enrollment.

Leave longer than two semesters requires readmission, which is treated as a new application and is not guaranteed.
