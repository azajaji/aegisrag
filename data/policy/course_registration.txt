Course registration for the fall semester opens on June 15 and closes on the first day of classes. Registration for the spring semester opens on November 15.

Add/drop period extends through the second week of classes; courses added after the first week require instructor consent and may not exceed the published course cap.

Maximum credit-hour load is 18 credit hours per semester for undergraduates and 12 credit hours for graduate students. Loads above these limits require written approval from the dean.

Course waitlists are maintained automatically; students are promoted to the roster in waitlist order as seats become available, with notifications sent by email.

Prerequisite overrides are issued by the department offering the course. Override requests must be submitted at least three business days before the add/drop deadline.

Audit registration carries the standard tuition cost; no grade is recorded for audited courses and audit status cannot be changed to credit after the first week.
