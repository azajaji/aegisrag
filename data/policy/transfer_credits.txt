Transfer credits are evaluated by the registrar in consultation with the department offering the equivalent course; only courses with a grade of C or higher are eligible.

Up to 60 credit hours may be transferred from another accredited institution toward an undergraduate degree; graduate transfer is capped at 12 credit hours.

Transfer credits do not contribute to the cumulative GPA but are listed on the transcript with the original grade reported as TR.

Credit by examination, including approved CLEP and AP credit, is granted only for scores above the published thresholds.

International transcripts must be submitted with a course-by-course evaluation from an approved credential evaluation service.
