The main library is open from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends during the regular semester. Hours are reduced during summer terms and holidays.

Undergraduate students may borrow up to ten books at a time for a loan period of 21 days. Graduate students may borrow up to twenty books for 60 days.

Books may be renewed twice online or by phone, provided no other patron has placed a hold on the item. After two renewals the book must be returned in person.

Overdue items accrue a fine of 0.50 SAR per day, capped at 50 SAR per item. Patrons with unpaid fines exceeding 100 SAR have their borrowing privileges suspended until cleared.

Group study rooms can be reserved up to seven days in advance using the library reservation system. Each reservation has a maximum duration of three hours per day per student.

Eating is prohibited in all library spaces; covered beverages are permitted in designated study areas but not in the special collections reading room.

Interlibrary loan requests typically take five to ten business days to fulfill. There is no fee for interlibrary loans for currently enrolled students or active faculty.
