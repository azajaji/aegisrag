Tuition refunds are issued according to a published schedule based on the official withdrawal date. Withdrawal within the first week qualifies for a 100 percent refund.

Withdrawal during the second week qualifies for a 75 percent refund of tuition. Course fees, technology fees, and laboratory fees are non-refundable after the first week of classes.

Withdrawal during the third or fourth week qualifies for a 50 percent refund. Withdrawals after the fourth week receive no tuition refund.

All refunds are credited to the original payment method within fifteen business days of the withdrawal being processed by the registrar.

Students receiving financial aid should consult the Financial Aid Office before withdrawing, since withdrawal may require partial repayment of aid disbursed.

International students must coordinate with the International Student Office to ensure withdrawal does not jeopardize visa status before the refund schedule is applied.
