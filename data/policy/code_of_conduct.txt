All members of the university community are expected to treat one another with respect, professionalism, and dignity in all interactions, both in person and online.

Harassment based on race, religion, gender, nationality, disability, or other protected characteristics is strictly prohibited and grounds for immediate disciplinary action.

Students are responsible for behavior during off-campus university activities including field trips, study-abroad programs, and conferences. Code of conduct violations during such activities follow the same procedures as on-campus violations.

Reports of misconduct may be submitted anonymously through the online reporting form. Anonymous reports are investigated to the extent possible given the limited information available.

Disciplinary sanctions range from written warnings to expulsion, depending on the severity and recurrence of the violation. Sanctions are recorded in the student's conduct file maintained by the Office of Student Affairs.
